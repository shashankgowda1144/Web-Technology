Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat with salt and pepper until mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil and warm it up.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs and spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Add cheese or veggies on top.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette and cook for a bit more.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with salt and pepper.
